Simple Workflow

From farm details to a bank-ready report in four steps

No spreadsheets, no manual maths. Fill the form section by section — the financial statements build themselves.

Enter Your Details

Name, contact, farm location and financing bank for the report cover.

Set Parameters

Animals, sheds, equipment, feed, labour and returns — guided, section by section.

Auto-Calculate

Investment, production, cash flow, repayment & depreciation compute instantly.

Get the Report

A complete, print-ready bankable project report with all viability ratios.

What you get

Everything a bank needs, calculated for you

Investment & Financing

Capital cost, working capital, bank loan, margin money and subsidy — itemised and totalled automatically.

7-Year Projections

Year-wise production, income, expenditure and cash flow, including staggered batch milk yields.

Repayment Schedule

Monthly EMI with moratorium period and a full 84-month amortisation table.

Viability Ratios

Net Present Value, Benefit–Cost Ratio, Internal Rate of Return and Debt Service Coverage Ratio.

Depreciation

Declining-balance depreciation on buildings (10%) and equipment (15%), carried across all years.

Print-Ready PDF

A clean, professionally formatted document you can save and submit to your bank.
